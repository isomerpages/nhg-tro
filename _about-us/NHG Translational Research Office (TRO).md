<p>The Translational Research Office (TRO) at NHG Group Research &amp; Innovation
(GRI) facilitates and supports translational research initiatives at NHG.
We work closely with partners such as the Lee Kong Chian School of Medicine
to establish joint institutes, programmes and projects that leverage NHG
Health's clinical strengths and our partners' complementary capabilities.
We also support the management of research agreements, grants and intellectual
property at NHG Health. This enables the generation of new knowledge and
insights towards better prevention, diagnosis, treatment and management
of diseases, as well as health maintenance, in alignment with NHG's vision
and mission.</p>
